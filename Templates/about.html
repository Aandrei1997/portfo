03 : About me

Who am I and what I am doing?

I am an engineer who works in Telecom and IT domain. I am providing second level maintenance for the Radio Access Network (RAN) for 2G, 3G and LTE technologies. Also I investigate and troubleshoot critical technical faults for the RAN network. I am using programming languages like Python to automate various scenarios in networks.

How did I get here?

I came to deal with this after studying at the Faculty of Electronics, Telecommunications and Information Technology, a faculty that is part of the Polytechnic University of Bucharest. Following my studies at college, I became passionate about telecommunications, but also about programming and software.

What am I looking for?

I am looking to improve my skills regarding programming area. Currently I know programming languages like Python, C, C++ and I want to become better at web programming, css, html, javascript. Following the realization of this project, I discovered that I also really like this part of the programming.
